What This Is

Our Game.
Our Way.

Aussie Rules footy is one of the most physical and fluid team sports going around. The physicality is a big part of what makes it special. The fluidity — the instinct, the improvisation, the ability to shift the play in a moment — is rare in any sport. We want to celebrate both.

This competition takes what we love about the classic game alongside what works in the modern game and builds something new from both. A summer league that gives players, fans, and communities a new way to enjoy footy.

What We Play

The Game As It
Should Be Played.

Player-led. Contest-driven. The rules are here to support the game, not interrupt it. Less whistle, more footy — it's as simple as that.

🏃
Player-Led Play
The game flows through the players, not the officials. Less interruption. More instinct. Let the athletes decide the moment.
💥
Embrace the Contest
Marking pressure reinstated. Win your position. Earn your mark. The contest is what makes this game what it is — and we're keeping it that way.
⚡
More Footy Per Minute
Every possession matters more. Every score swings harder. Shorter matches, same game — the pressure is there from the first bounce.
🎯
Consistent and Fair
Decisions that match the play — proportional and readable, so players and fans both know where they stand.
🧠
Skill Over System
Structures built around the players. Smaller rosters mean more game time, more involvement, and more of the footy we actually want to watch.
🌊
Celebrate the Fluidity
The run-and-carry, the instinctive handball, the unpredictable bounce — the things that make this game like nothing else on the field. We build around them — and take them with us.

Scoring

Exciting
New Scoring.

A scoring system that rewards accuracy and guts. Every kick has real consequences — there's no safe option, no easy fallback. Be precise. Be brave.

6
Goal — Big Sticks
Split the big posts, take the six. Classic goal, classic value.
−1
Behind — It Costs You
A behind subtracts a point. Accuracy is rewarded. Missing wide has real consequences.
½
Match Duration
Half the length of a standard match. Two halves. Concentrated and decided.
3
Overtime — First to Three Goals
Level at the siren? First team to kick three goals wins. Pure pressure footy.
