What This Is

Our Game.
Our Way.

Aussie Rules footy is one of the most physical and fluid team sports going around. The physicality is a big part of what makes it special. The fluidity — the instinct, the improvisation, the ability to shift the play in a moment — is rare in any sport. We celebrate both. We promote both.

This competition takes what we love about the classic game alongside what works in the modern game and builds something new from both. A summer league that gives players, fans, and communities a new way to enjoy footy.

The Game Philosophy
What We Play

The Game As It
Should Be Played.

Like the games of old — strong and bold. Player-led. Contest-driven. The rules are here to support the game, not interrupt it. Less whistle, more footy.

🏃
Play-On
The game flows through the players, not the officials. Less interruption. More instinct. Let the athletes decide the moment.
💥
Embrace the Contest
Marking pressure reinstated. Win your position. Earn your mark. The contest is what makes this game what it is — and we're keeping it that way.
⚡
More Footy Per Minute
Shorter matches, same game — concentrated into two halves where the pressure builds from the first bounce and stays there.
🎯
Consistent and Fair
Decisions that match the play — proportional and readable, so players know where they stand and matches aren't swayed by minor infractions.
🧠
Skill Over System
Built around the players — because that's the best part of the game. Structures that let talent shine, decisions that match the play, and a stage where footy people can become footy names.
🌊
Celebrate the Fluidity
The run-and-carry, the instinctive handball, the unpredictable bounce — these travel. The game can go anywhere, and so can they.
½
Half the time. Same game.
Two halves. Shorter matches, same footy — every kick counts, every score shifts the game.
